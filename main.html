    About Amoko's Sprout

    At AMOKO'S SPROUT, we are passionate about adding value to cassava and other local produce. We take pride in producing and supplying high-quality cassava-based products made from locally grown roots, as well as helping reduce the drudgery involved in weeding cassava farms. We educate and empower farmers to improve productivity and efficiency. See more.

    Quick Agricultural Tips

    Rotate crops to improve soil fertility.
    Use organic fertilizers to reduce costs.
    Check cassava roots for pests before harvest.

    Our Products Overview

    Fresh Cassava Tubers, naturally grown, high-yield,
    and ideal for both domestic and industrial use.

    Our Yellow Garri is enriched with natural palm oil

    Our Local Semo Flour is a finely milled, fast-cooking cassava-based
