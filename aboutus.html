About Amoko's Sprout

At AMOKO'S SPROUT, we are passionate about adding value
to cassava and other local produce.We take pride in producing
and supplying high-quality cassava-based products made from
locally grown roots as well as help reduce the drudgery involved
in weeding cassava farms.We educate and empower individuals
to process agricultural products locally;From garri and fufu flour
to cassava starch and tapioca, each product is carefully processed
to meet the highest standards of taste,hygiene, and nutrition.
Through sustainable farming, training, and quality product sales,
we help communities create income,reduce waste, and promote
food security.Together, we’re building a future where every harvest
counts and local agriculture thrives.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring you healthy, affordable, and naturally processed
foods that support local farmers and promote sustainable agriculture.
Whether you’re buying for home use, resale, or large-scale supply,
Amoko's Sprout, is your trusted source for premium cassava products—
fresh from our farms to your table.

Our Vision

To become a leading cassava solutions provider in Africa and beyond.
